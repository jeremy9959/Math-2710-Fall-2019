Mathematics

  • Mathematical Reasoning by Ted Sundstrom is another textbook that is available for free and is a useful resource.

  • The Book of Proof by Richard Hammack is another freely available text. We will refer to the discussion of sequences in this text.

  • Russell’s Paradox is a famous observation of Bertrand Russell that illustrated that naive notions of “sets” lead to logical contradictions. Famously, Russell’s work destroyed the life work of Frege who had hoped to reduce arithmetic to logic.
